Mutant Variant - Pandemic Returns

July 07 , 2021

Time : 12:17 PM

Mutant variants are rising day by day and we have to be secure and healthy.

Genetic Variant of sars COV 2 have been roaming around now and throught the world.
These variants seems to be not stoppable until unless we take strong actions and measures. As of now the vaccination has started in fast pace to create a strong antibody inside so we could fight those mutants.

Some noteable variants are -
1. B.1.617.2 DELTA VARIANT
2. LAMBDA VARIANT
3. DELTA PLUS VARIANT
4.B.1.1.7 VARIANT[Alpha]
5. B.1.351 [Gamma]

These Mutated variants are a huge factor which should be eradicated otherwise We May have to face pandemic of higher intensity
Government have already controlled the delta plus variant , still cases are rising . Being home and getting vaccination is a way to be secure
Other ways by which we could safe guard ourselves include -
1. Wearing Mask Always when going out .
2. Avoid getting too close
3. Get vaccinated ASAP
WHAT ARE THE CONCERNS ??


The Concerns relate to their fast transmissibilty , severity of disease , neutralising capacity , and the age group it would affect in general . Some Concerns on How it is going to act on recovered patients .

All these questions are being studied daily and reaserch is going on extensively. So we a People should avoid unncessary travel now a days, and increse our immunity .
